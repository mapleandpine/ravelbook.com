Author Bio

More information about the author, Nicholas J. Marson.

Hi, I'm Nic

I figure you don't really care that I live in Portland, OR or what my favourite color is, so here are some other facts about me instead:

  • I wish I had a pet bear. You could probably learn to ride it and it would eat people who annoy you. I would call him Captain Grumpy.
  • If I was marooned on a desert island I'd try to survive by eating my own leg. That way I'd get a delicious, tasty leg-roast and I'd have one less limb to support.
  • When I was 12, I freaked out a girl I liked by following her around for an entire school dance. If you're reading this Ingrid, sorry for the 'crazed murderer stalking his prey' vibe; I was just trying to ask you out.
  • Lamingtons may be the best dessert ever invented. Do you have them where you are? They're awesome.

My favorite colour is orange.

I love nice comments (because I respond to praise like a dog), but I love constructive criticism even more. If you notice things in the story that aren't quite working I would really like to hear from you!

I guess that makes you pretty important.

No pressure.

Plans for the future

Write! Publish! Repeat! :) I will be publishing four books in 2015 and four more in 2016. I also have plans for a short story collection in the future, as well as a few parodies. How fun!

When I'm not writing

I spend time with my family, work on many of my other projects, and keep up with my reading. I currently live in Portland, Oregon with my wife Natalie and our little girl, River.

I paddle outrigger canoes

The Bridge City Paddling Club is kind enough to have me as a member of their awesome outrigger canoe team. We practice on the Willamette River and race in Oregon and Washington.

I'm a web developer

Visit my other website, Nic Marson, for more information. I work at ConnectiveDX, an agence that specializes in Digital Experiences.

Read or listen to lots of books and watch movies and TV

Some of my favorite books are: Wool, Ready Player One, A Diamond Age, Pathfinder, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Game of Thrones, The Stormlight Archive, and Slaughterhouse Five.

Some of my favorite movies are: A New Hope (1977), Empire Strikes Back (1980), Return of the Jedi (1983), Disney's Alice in Wonderland (1951), The Matrix (1999), Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter.
